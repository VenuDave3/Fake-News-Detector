News Interpreter

A lightweight demo that performs two tasks: automatic news summarization, and a fake-vs-real classifier built using NLP and scikit-learn. Use the demo to paste article text or provide a URL and get a short summary plus predicted labels.

How it works (brief)

  • Generates short extractive summaries using a compact sentence-scoring approach.
